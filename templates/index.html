Understanding the Analysis

Each grid point represents a potential home location. For each location, we calculated:

  • 1,560 travel times - every minute from 6am-7pm, both to and from work
  • 80th percentile - the score shown, representing slower commute times
  • Bus frequency matters - frequent buses show better (lower) scores
